Cult of Hela (Page WIP)

This project is in a Vertical Slice state.

3C

Camera

This game is played with a 2/3 top camera view. The camera will hide elements between itself and the character.

Character

The character contains multiple elements :

  • Basic Attack :
      Damages calculated with stats & weapons
  • Spells :
      It can be offensive, defensive or utility and it's calculated by stats
  • Stats :
      Strength : increase you basic attack damages
      Intelligence : increase your spell damages
      Dexterity : increase your critical strike damages
      Vitality : increase your health
      Spirit : increase your mana
  • Equipment slots :
      Head, Body, Leggings, Boots, Necklace, 2 Rings, Trinket, Right Hand, Left Hand
  • Inventory :
      40 slots of inventory
      Golds

    • Controller

    The character can be moved by a Right-Click, it will search for a path to travel (using Unity pathfinding).

    You can press A,Z,E,R to cast binded spells in your Spellbar.

    Left-Click makes you attack an enemy with a Basic Attack.

    You can press C to open your Inventory and V to open you Spell book

    Game UI

    Inventory

    You have 3 sections :

  • Character Statistics Sheet :
      Here you can see your level, experience, and all your statistics.
      If you hover with your mouse over a statistics written in yellow, you will have a quick explanation of what it's doing to your character (top of the screen)
  • Equipments :
      Here you can see what equipment you have currently on your character. You can drag and drop an object from your inventory to put it in the slot. If you select an item in your inventory, adapted slots will be white, otherwise red. Right-Click on an object in your Inventory will auto-equip it in the right slot and switch the currently equipped item.
  • Character's Bag :
      When you Right-Click on an object on the ground, it will be put in your bag. If you put your mouse over an item, it will show a description of it (right-side of the screen).
      At the bottom-right, you have your gold amount.

    • Spellbook

    For this version, you have 5 different spells you can equip plus your Basic Attack.

    If you put your mouse over a spell or the basic attack, it will show a description of it.

    Left-Click on a spell will equip/remove it from your spell bar in your Overlay.


    Overlay

  • Bottom Right :
      Red Bar : Your Health
      Blue Bar : Your Mana
      4 spell slots you have binded
      1 Basic-Attack slot (it's planned to have a different one in the future)
  • Bottom-Right :
      Yellow Bar : Your Experience until the next level
      C : Inventory button
      V : Spellbook button
      ESC : button planned to open the menu
